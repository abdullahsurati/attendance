Attendance Recorder

Welcome to Attendance Recorder.

Attendance Recorder is a project that I have taken which does the obvious work of recording your attendance.

Initially, I will creating attendance table for SEM III of my S.Y.Bsc.IT course in my college. So all the subjects will be according to my course.

If you want to track your attendance, you must login, and then you can record and track your attendance.
